About Institution

Government ITI is leading educational organisation in India.

In a developing Country like India, witnessing a revolutionary up-graduation in literacy, demand of skilled technician is also bound to grow. It is important on the part if vivational training institution, to grow with the same phase for the betterment of the indudtry, society, as well as overall industrial growth of the nation.

Vocational training is an important subject under constitution. The central as well as the state government share the responsibility of implementing vocational training in the country.

  • ITI is a Technical course one can join after successful completion of class 10th or Matriculation.
  • ITI is a great course for students who want to do a professional technical course in the short term.
  • ITI is a good job oriented Technical course.
  • ITI holder can get a good job in electrical, mechanical, and other manufacturing sectors.
  • So many students are getting Jobs in railways nowadays after ITI.

We OFFER best of best Education

As you know how the ITI course has been designed so that technical education can be given to as many people as possible.

  • ITI courses are available which you can join even after the 10th.
  • 12th pass students can also Join the ITI course.
  • For most of the courses, the minimum age at the time of admission should not be less than 14 years.
  • The maximum age should be below 40 years. Age relaxation for reserved quota students is also available.
  • For most of the state government ITI colleges, you have to appear and qualify in the different sate ITI entrance exams.

Therefore the duration of the ITI course also varies, some courses can be of 6 months, 9 months, one year or even 2 years. Most courses after which you can do engineering or diploma, the duration of the course is 2 years.

Govt. Jobs ITI

After doing ITI, you can get a lot of government jobs. Initially, this post will be elementary, but later on, you can take a lot of promotions and reach a good position.

Private Jobs after ITI

After doing ITI, you have the option of a lot of private jobs, which you can choose according to your branch and interest.Many students who get placement through campus selection, get jobs in private sectors, in the beginning salary may be fewer. Still, with time and experience, you will get a good salary.

Higher education After ITI

If a student wants to graduate after ITI, then he has to do twelfth. Or the other option is that with his ITI course, if he gets plus two from NIOS, then he can also graduate from NIOS after completing his ITI course.
